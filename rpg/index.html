Fantasy Worlds Ain't Flat Adventuring Society

Tabletop RPG Adventures

I'm a huge fan of tabletop roleplaying games. Since late 2017, I've been part of the Fantasy Worlds Ain't Flat Adventuring Society - a group of friends exploring new and intriguing worlds with a sense of aplomb and a dash of derring-do.

Below you'll find campaign journals, reference sheets, and other gaming resources from our adventures.

Campaign Journals

Symbaroum

A dark fantasy RPG set in the ancient forest of Davokar. Our adventure journals document our party's exploration of this dangerous and mysterious world.

Symbaroum Adventure Journals

Mouse Guard

Based on the beloved comic series, we play as brave mice defending their communities from predators and the elements. Mice with swords - what's not to love?

Mouse Guard Adventure Journals

Shadowrun

Cyberpunk meets fantasy in this classic RPG. Our campaigns have taken us from fighting Elder Gods to stopping rogue AIs in the neon-lit streets of the Sixth World.
