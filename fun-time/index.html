How Costs Are Calculated

Once we have confirmed commitments, we'll check actual prices and divide the total using this formula:

Unit System

  • Adults (age 17+): 1 unit
  • Youth/Kids (age 16 and under): ½ unit
Total Price ÷ Number of Units = Per Unit Price

Example Calculation

Total Cost: $4,200
Attendance: 9 adults, 7 kids

$4,200 ÷ ((9 × 2) + 7) = $4,200 ÷ 25 half-units = $168 per half-unit

Your family (2 adults + 4 kids = 8 half-units):

8 × $168 = $1,344 total
Down Payment: 50% of your final amount
(In the example: $672 would be due February 18)
